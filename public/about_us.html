who we are

BEFOED - Beyond Foreign Education is a top consultant for international education, providing aspirant students with the necessary advice to realise their ambition of studying abroad.

With a team that is both highly competent and experienced, BEFOED - Beyond Foreign Education began operating in 2016.

Beyond Foreign Education is a trusted partner in turning your study abroad dreams into reality. With expertise in global education consulting, we provide personalized support to help students navigate the complex process of pursuing education at world-renowned institutions.
