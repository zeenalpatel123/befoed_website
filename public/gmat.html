Overview

  • The Graduate Management Admission Test, or GMAT, is typically taken by MBA students who want to study overseas.

  • The test is scored out of a total of 800 and score is valid for duration of 5 years.

  • Students can appear for a reattempt for up to 5 times in any 12-month period, but no more than once in any 16-day period.

  • GMAT exam scores are used by more than 3,000 graduate management programs throughout the world and is also accepted by selected Indian B-Schools.

  • This exam assesses applicants' Integrated Reasoning skills, Quantitative skills, Verbal skills, and Analytical Writing Assessment.
