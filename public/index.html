About Us

We are making a significant investment in your path to success.

Since 2016, BEFOED - Beyond Foreign Education has stood as a prominent advisor for individuals aspiring to pursue careers abroad. Our highly skilled team provides reliable assistance, recruiting students for 500+ universities worldwide, spanning the United States, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, the United Kingdom, Dubai, Ireland, and various European and Asian nations. Tailoring options to academic and professional needs, we've successfully guided over 5000 students. Notably, we are proud to state that we are actively recruiting students from 16 different countries across South Asia, Middle East and South East Asia, and Africa. BEFOED - Beyond Foreign Education is dedicated to growth and excellence in the upcoming 5 years.
